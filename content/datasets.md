## Datasets

- [ColoRadar](/coloradar/) — 52 sequences of 3D FMCW radar, lidar, and IMU data in mines, built environments, and urban creek paths.
- [ColoRadar+](/coloradarplus/) — extension of ColoRadar with improved ground truth odometry and RGB-D camera data.
- [OIVIO](/oivio/) — visual-inertial odometry sequences with onboard illumination in mines, tunnels, and dark environments.
- [CU-Multi](/cumulti/) — a dataset for multi-robot data association.
- [Canyonlands](/canyonlands_dataset/) — field robotics dataset from the Canyonlands region.
- [MARBLE SubT Finals](/marble/) — sensor data recorded on the DARPA SubT Finals competition course.
- [SUN-Spot](/sunspot/) — RGB-D dataset with spatial referring expressions.

## Projects

- [SceneSense](/scenesense/) — diffusion models for 3D occupancy synthesis from partial observation.
- [RMap](/rmap/) — millimeter-wave radar mapping through volumetric upsampling.
- [RestoreBot](/restorebot/) — robotic systems for restoring degraded rangelands.
- [Verified Control of Cooperative Autonomous Vehicles](/cps-1646556/) — guaranteed-safe control algorithms for autonomous vehicles.
